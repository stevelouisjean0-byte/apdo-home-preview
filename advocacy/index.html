Advocacy

Lawful, bipartisan engagement built on research and relationships.

APDO is designed to provide a consistent, professional voice on U.S.-Haiti issues through policy education, coalition-building, and responsible civic engagement.

How APDO advocates

The advocacy program separates serious public policy work from slogans. It is built to inform, brief, convene, and mobilize responsibly.

  • Congressional and agency education
  • Coalition engagement with civic and business leaders
  • Public education campaigns
  • Policy letters and briefing materials
  • Chapter-based civic mobilization

Bipartisan

Focused on the relationship and the issues, not candidate endorsements.

Evidence-based

Grounded in research, expert input, and clearly stated priorities.

Compliant

Activities should be conducted in compliance with applicable federal, state, and local law.
