About APDO

A serious national voice for a stronger U.S.-Haiti partnership.

APDO is being formed to bring continuity, credibility, and professional advocacy to a relationship shaped by history, community, commerce, democratic values, and shared strategic interest.

Why this organization exists

More than a million Haitian Americans contribute across medicine, business, public service, the arts, education, and civic life. APDO exists to turn that community strength into organized, lawful, bipartisan policy influence.

Institutional posture

APDO is an organization in formation. Legal status, contribution policies, membership dues, and formal governance details will be announced as filings and organizational setup are completed.

Core functions

Research

Independent, non-partisan analysis that helps policymakers and civic leaders understand the issues clearly.

Advocacy

Lawful, bipartisan engagement focused on evidence-based policy and long-term partnership.

Convening

Bringing together policymakers, investors, scholars, business leaders, and civic organizations.
