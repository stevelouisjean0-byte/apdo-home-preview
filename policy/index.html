Policy priorities

Evidence-based priorities for a durable bilateral relationship.

APDO's policy work is designed to be serious, bipartisan, and grounded in democratic institutions, economic opportunity, public safety, and responsible partnership.

Democratic institutions

Support for accountable governance, civic participation, institutional capacity, and democratic resilience.

Economic opportunity

Trade, investment, entrepreneurship, workforce development, and responsible private-sector engagement.

Security and stability

Practical policy discussion around public safety, rule of law, humanitarian needs, and regional coordination.

Diaspora leadership

Elevating Haitian-American expertise, civic participation, professional networks, and public leadership.
